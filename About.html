About






We are a team of high school students from the Bay Area, passionate about the interdisciplinary areas within the biomedical engineering field. Originally, this club was founded by a group of students with two shared interests: some of us were drawn to the medical field, while others were passionate about technology. Biomedical Engineering became the natural bridge between these interests, inspiring us to form a community where both perspectives could come together to learn, collaborate, and innovate.


Through countless months of effort, we’ve shaped our club’s identity through a defined mission, cohesive visuals, and hands-on initiatives centered on collaboration and impact. Through projects, workshops, and outreach, we aim to tell a story rooted in interdisciplinary learning and growth. We hope that with this website, visitors can learn more about our mission, explore our work, and become part of our growing community.


To learn more about our team, visit our Meet The Team page.
