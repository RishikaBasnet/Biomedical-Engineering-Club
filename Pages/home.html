Educating Students on the Intergration of Technology in Medicine

The Biomedical Engineering Club is a community of curious and driven students passionate about the intersection of medicine, technology, and innovation. Our purpose is to explore how engineering principles can be applied to improve healthcare and human well-being. Through hands-on projects and collaborative discussions, we aim to help members discover their interests in fields such as medicine, computer science, and biomedical design. Our goal is to inspire students to pursue their passions while gaining insight into real-world biomedical challenges and advancements.







Meetings take place in room D201 at Alameda High School in Alameda, California biweekly.


Not Affiliated with Alameda High School? Find more information about biomedical engineering on our Fields, Resources, and Opportunities pages.
